About Me

Coming from Western Massachusetts, I chose UMass Boston for its renowned IT program and the opportunity to live in the bustling city atmosphere, a refreshing change from the slower pace back home, all while pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree.

I am passionate about engaging in projects similar to this website, immersing myself in the intricacies of technology. I aspire to expand my knowledge and advocate for the cultivation of robust, ethical code to foster a healthier and more respectable digital environment.

Outside of my college career, I actively engage with my community through involvement in the Social Justice club, volunteering with the Elizabeth Freeman Center, and participating in diversity fairs to make a positive impact.

When class isn't in session, I enjoy exploring Boston with friends, attending sports events like the Bruins and Celtics, and indulging in my favorite shows and mangas: Solo Leveling, One Piece, Jujutsu Kaisen, and Kaiju No.8.
